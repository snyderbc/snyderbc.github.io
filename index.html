Top Austin Music Venues

With it's "Live Music Capital of the World" status, navigating Austin's music scene can be overwhelming. Here we are highlighting the top local venues, whether you're looking to catch an old favorite on their stop through town, or discover the next Gary Clark Jr.

1. Stubb's

Nestled in the heart of the Red River District, Stubb's is known for two things: Bar-B-Q and incredible live music. With an indoor and outdoor stage, you'll find everything from big names to local unknowns. Take a look at their upcoming shows here.
